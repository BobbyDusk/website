Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how our Pinterest Integration App for Postiz ("the App") handles, stores, and protects user data authorized through the Pinterest Developer Platform.

1. Scopes and Data Accessed

When connecting your Pinterest account to Postiz via our integration, the App requests permission to look at and manage specified structural elements through OAuth:

  • Profile Information: Username, bio, and profile image to identify your workspace channel within Postiz.
  • Boards & Pins Data: Structural details of your Pinterest boards to enable target destination mapping, and Pin text/image data sent for content generation.
  • OAuth Tokens: Read and write access credentials required to interface directly with Pinterest endpoints on your behalf.

2. Data Handling Practices

The retrieved data is strictly limited to operations initiated by you within your Postiz instance (e.g., publishing a Pin or fetching board lists). Because this code runs locally inside your self-hosted or cloud instance of Postiz, your analytics, tokens, and asset configurations never leave your architecture, except when securely sent directly to Pinterest's developer API servers.

3. Disclosure & Security

We do not lease, trade, or distribute your account identifiers or access tokens to any external entity. All API requests are run over transport layers utilizing standard HTTPS encryption parameters.

4. Data Revocation and Removal

You can instantly cut permissions and purge any integration state data stored inside your application environment:

  • Disconnecting the Pinterest channel profile interface within your Postiz account configuration panel.
  • Navigating to your native Pinterest security configuration under Settings > Security and Logins > Apps and choosing to revoke access to this app. Doing so instantly renders the app tokens obsolete.

5. Amendments

This privacy declaration may be revised periodically. Any relevant modifications will be indicated by the date stamp at the head of the statement.

6. Contact

For concerns regarding integration privacy logic, please contact the local technical administrator of your Postiz workspace environment.
