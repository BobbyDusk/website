Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our TikTok Integration App for Dusky Postiz ("the App") handles, processes, and protects your data when you connect your TikTok account via the TikTok Developer Platform.

1. Information We Collect

Because this App acts as a direct conduit between your Dusky Postiz instance and the TikTok API, we treat your privacy with the utmost respect. The App accesses the following information via OAuth scope permissions:

  • TikTok Profile Information: Basic public profile details (username, display name, avatar) to identify your connected channel inside Dusky Postiz.
  • Authentication Tokens: Access and Refresh Tokens provided securely by TikTok to allow Dusky Postiz to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Video files, descriptions, and scheduling times configured by you inside Dusky Postiz for publication to TikTok.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely to facilitate the core functions of the App:

  • To authenticate your identity and authorize video uploads via official TikTok APIs.
  • To publish videos directly to your chosen TikTok account as scheduled within Dusky Postiz.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Your data is transferred exclusively to TikTok for publishing. If you are running a self-hosted instance of Dusky Postiz, your data resides entirely on your server infrastructure and is never transmitted to us or any external party other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

4. Data Retention

Authentication tokens are stored securely within your Dusky Postiz database environment. They are retained as long as your TikTok account remains connected to Dusky Postiz. You can revoke access at any time, which immediately removes or invalidates these tokens.

5. Revoking Access and User Rights

You have full control over your data. You can disconnect the integration and delete all stored tokens instantly by:

  • Removing the TikTok channel connection inside your Dusky Postiz dashboard settings.
  • Revoking permissions directly via your TikTok account security settings under "Authorized Apps".

6. Security Measures

We leverage official industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols provided by the TikTok Developer Platform to securely connect accounts. It is your responsibility to ensure your Dusky Postiz environment uses secure connection methods (such as HTTPS) to protect tokens during transmission.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

For data inquiries or assistance with your setup's data deletion practices, please contact your local Dusky Postiz administrator.
