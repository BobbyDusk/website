Postiz-bobbydusk

An automated social media deployment app used to manage, schedule, and safely automate video publishing directly to your TikTok feed.

App Functionality

Postiz-bobbydusk connects an independent, self-hosted Postiz software instance directly to the TikTok Developer Platform. Designed specifically for content creators managing unique personal workflows, this integration ensures absolute data control while maintaining reliable social media consistency.

Automated Scheduling

Draft your creative captions and arrange your video calendar natively inside Postiz, letting our server deploy posts exactly when your audience is active.

Direct Video Uploads

Bypass manual mobile transfers. Seamlessly stage, transfer, and push high-definition video assets straight to your TikTok profile using official Webhooks.

Secure Token Security

Leveraging secure industry-standard OAuth 2.0 architectures, credentials map directly to your localized client database environment.

API Usage & Requested Data

To perform automated commands efficiently, this application utilizes authorized credentials to request specific platform access scopes:

  • user.info.basic: Used to display your authenticated user avatar and profile handle inside your local Postiz channel dashboard.
  • video.upload / video.publish: Used exclusively to deliver your scheduled video binaries, video metadata, and descriptive captions directly into your TikTok creator feed.
