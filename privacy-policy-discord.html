Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy documents how our Discord Bot Integration for Postiz ("the Bot") accesses, treats, and maintains server data authorized through the Discord Developer Portal.

1. Information Accessed and Handled

To securely pipe workspace post updates into your server, the Bot requests distinct Discord OAuth2 / Bot application permissions:

  • Guild/Server Metadata: Unique server identification numbers, channel titles, and category paths to help map where your Postiz notifications should print.
  • Bot Identity & Tokens: Secured token sets deployed locally inside your environment to safely call Discord endpoints.
  • Message Delivery Payloads: Text strings, hyperlink URLs, thumbnail assets, and media components configured inside Postiz to generate the final chat embeds.

Note: This integration does NOT track, read, or cache chat logs, user text patterns, or personal profile data across your channels. It operates purely as a directional outbound broadcast assistant.

2. Processing Context

Data routing remains exclusively functional to satisfy notifications you program. Because this application is written to deploy locally on your self-hosted or dedicated cloud system infrastructure, no logs are outsourced or shared with central indexing servers. Transfers take place explicitly over TLS-guarded lanes connecting your machine to official Discord gateway servers.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not capture, trade, or distribute server identifiers, channel structures, or configurations to marketing pipelines or third-party networks.

4. Data Retention & Erasure

Configuration hooks run continuously within your database stack as long as the Bot remains installed. You can delete authorization logs and permanently wipe stored parameters at your discretion:

  • Deleting the specific Discord destination feed out of your local Postiz account panel.
  • Right-clicking the Bot profile within your server configuration hierarchy and selecting Kick Bot or Ban Bot. Dropping the bot terminates gateway connectivity and renders database authentication payloads completely useless.

5. Policy Revisions

This document may change as Discord alters developer guidelines. Revised definitions will carry an adjusted timeline stamp at the top of this content view.

6. Technical Contact

For operations concerning server data privacy controls, please connect directly with the administrator running your Postiz core environment.
